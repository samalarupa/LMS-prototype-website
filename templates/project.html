Face Recognition Attendence

Facial recognition attendance eliminates proxy punching, a problem with traditional methods. It verifies users in real-time using facial features, ensuring accurate attendance.

Quiz

Learning websites pack a double punch with quizzes: assess understanding and solidify learning. Quizzes test knowledge, then retrieval practice during quizzes strengthens memory.

Notes

Taking notes engages your brain, filtering information and boosting memory. They improve focus and solidify learning for later recall, acting as a bridge between passive learning and active understanding.
